Managed Servers

As EPMVirtual team, we provide two separate type of maintenance plans:

  • Managed Servers
  • Unmanaged Servers

A managed server means that, the EPMVirtual team has the full responsibility of your server. This includes applying patches for your server, updating your MS Windows and MS Office applications, managing security and credentials, etc.

When you own a managed server you still have the total ownership rights on the server, with the freedom of focusing your own work. Because we take care of all the hassle of patching, updating and managing.

Server management fee is $200/month, and you can activate server management service by opening a support ticket after you subscribe to any of our server packages.
